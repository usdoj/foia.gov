under the Freedom of Information Act (FOIA).

transmit spam, threats, phishing content, or other malicious or abusive material
is prohibited and constitutes a violation of these Terms of Service.
Similarly, malicious or abusive behavior on this site is prohibited and
constitutes a violation of these Terms of Service. If you submit information
that includes a credible threat or appears to involve unlawful activity,
we may disclose that information to appropriate law enforcement authorities.

Federal agencies may evaluate user conduct on FOIA.gov for compliance with
these Terms of Service, consistent with applicable statutes and each agency’s
implementing regulations.  Improper use of this system may affect the processing
of your submission, including potential delays or limitations on the use of
FOIA.gov for submitting requests. Nothing in these Terms alters any agency’s
legal obligations under FOIA.

More information on the Department of Justice’s privacy policies can be found
[here](https://www.justice.gov/doj/privacy-policy). Additional legal policies
